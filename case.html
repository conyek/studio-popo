Feedo

3-day-intensive hackathon project (awarded 2nd prize)

background issue

We were team of 3 (1 designer, 2 developers), squeezing our brains to think about achievable mini project for the hackathon. While talking about the everyday issues at work, we landed on the issue that as the user of our own products/ services, sometimes we have our suggestions and feedback but it’s troublesome to communicate our ideas. And mainly it’s difficult because it’s hard to find who’s the right person to talk to, and also from that person’s perspective, it’s difficult to track back that suggestion from emails or chat.
